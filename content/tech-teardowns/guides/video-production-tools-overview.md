# Video Production Tools Overview

**Purpose**: Overview of tools and dashboards that video creators use for managing production workflows.

---

## Popular Tools Used by Video Creators

### 1. Notion ⭐ (Most Popular)

**What It Is**: All-in-one workspace with databases, pages, and templates

**Why Creators Love It**:
- **Flexible**: Can organize anything (content calendar, scripts, research, tasks)
- **Free Tier**: Generous free plan
- **Templates**: Lots of free YouTube/content creator templates
- **All-in-One**: One place for everything (no switching tools)

**How Creators Use It**:
- Content calendar with video ideas
- Project pages for each video
- Research databases
- Script writing
- Task management
- Asset organization

**Best For**: Solo creators or small teams who want everything in one place

**Cost**: Free (personal), $8/month (Plus), $15/month (Business)

**Example Setup**:
- Database of video projects
- Content calendar view
- Templates for scripts, research
- Linked pages for each project

---

### 2. Trello

**What It Is**: Kanban board (cards in columns)

**Why Creators Love It**:
- **Visual**: See everything at a glance
- **Simple**: Easy to understand and use
- **Free**: Free tier is very capable
- **Mobile**: Good mobile app

**How Creators Use It**:
- Columns: Ideas → Research → Filming → Editing → Published
- Cards: One card per video
- Checklists: Tasks within each card
- Labels: Categories, priorities

**Best For**: Visual thinkers, simple workflows

**Cost**: Free (basic), $5/month (Standard), $10/month (Premium)

**Example Setup**:
```
[Ideas] → [Research] → [Filming] → [Editing] → [Published]
```

---

### 3. Airtable

**What It Is**: Spreadsheet + database (relational)

**Why Creators Love It**:
- **Relational**: Link videos to research, assets, etc.
- **Views**: Calendar, kanban, gallery views
- **Free Tier**: 1,000 records/year free
- **Powerful**: More than a spreadsheet

**How Creators Use It**:
- Video database with all projects
- Link to research sources
- Link to assets (images, files)
- Calendar view for publishing
- Gallery view for visual planning

**Best For**: Creators who need to link related data

**Cost**: Free (1,000 records), $12/month (Plus), $24/month (Pro)

**Example Setup**:
- Videos table (linked to)
- Research table
- Assets table
- Calendar view for schedule

---

### 4. Asana

**What It Is**: Project management with tasks and projects

**Why Creators Love It**:
- **Structured**: Good for teams and workflows
- **Templates**: Content calendar templates
- **Automation**: Can automate workflows
- **Integrations**: Lots of integrations

**How Creators Use It**:
- Projects for each video
- Tasks with dependencies
- Content calendar
- Team collaboration

**Best For**: Teams, structured workflows

**Cost**: Free (basic), $10.99/month (Premium), $24.99/month (Business)

---

### 5. ClickUp

**What It Is**: All-in-one project management

**Why Creators Love It**:
- **Creator-Focused**: Built for content creators
- **Views**: Multiple views (list, board, calendar)
- **Templates**: YouTube creator templates
- **Free Tier**: Good free plan

**How Creators Use It**:
- Projects for video series
- Tasks for each video
- Calendar for publishing
- Docs for scripts/research

**Best For**: Creators who want structure but flexibility

**Cost**: Free (basic), $7/month (Unlimited), $12/month (Business)

---

### 6. StudioBinder

**What It Is**: Professional video production software

**Why Creators Love It**:
- **Professional**: Used by production companies
- **Comprehensive**: Scripts, storyboards, scheduling
- **Industry Standard**: Trusted by professionals

**How Creators Use It**:
- Script writing and breakdown
- Shot lists and storyboards
- Production scheduling
- Team collaboration

**Best For**: Professional productions, larger teams

**Cost**: $29/month (Starter), $49/month (Professional)

---

### 7. Clipflow

**What It Is**: Purpose-built for content creators

**Why Creators Love It**:
- **Creator-Focused**: Built specifically for creators
- **Unified**: Concept to publishing in one place
- **Automation**: Automatic task assignment
- **Multi-Channel**: Manage multiple channels

**How Creators Use It**:
- Project pipeline from idea to publish
- Automatic task assignments
- Multi-channel coordination
- Reduced tool switching

**Best For**: Content creators who want specialized tools

**Cost**: Contact for pricing

## What Most Creators Actually Use

### Solo Creators / Small Teams
1. **Notion** (most popular) - All-in-one flexibility
2. **Trello** - Simple kanban boards
3. **Airtable** - If they need relational data

### Teams / Larger Operations
1. **Asana** - Structured workflows
2. **ClickUp** - All-in-one with team features
3. **StudioBinder** - Professional production

```

```


### Pattern 3: Airtable Base

```
Videos Table
├── Linked to Research Table
```

## Recommendations for Tech Teardown Videos

### If You Want Simple
**Use**: Trello
- One board
- Columns for each phase
- Cards for each device
- Checklists in cards

### If You Want Everything in One Place
**Use**: Notion
- Database of teardown projects
- Linked research pages
- Script templates
- Content calendar

### If You Need to Link Data
**Use**: Airtable
- Videos table
- Research sources table (linked)
- Components table (linked)
- Calendar view

### If You Want Professional Tools
**Use**: StudioBinder or ClickUp
- More features
- Better for teams
- More structured
