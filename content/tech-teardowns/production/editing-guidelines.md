## Video Editing

### Basic Editing

#### Cutting
- **Purpose**: Remove mistakes, tighten pacing
- **When**: Remove errors, long pauses, unnecessary sections
- **How**: Use cut tool, trim clips
- **Keep**: Best takes, smooth flow

#### Trimming
- **Purpose**: Tighten timing, remove dead space
- **When**: Beginning/end of clips, between actions
- **How**: Trim handles, remove frames
- **Keep**: Natural flow, not too tight

#### Sequencing
- **Purpose**: Arrange clips in logical order
- **Order**: Follow script/story structure
- **Flow**: Smooth transitions between sections
- **Pacing**: Maintain momentum

### Transitions

#### Types of Transitions

**Cut** (Most Common)
- **When**: Standard scene changes
- **Effect**: Instant change
- **Use**: Most transitions

**Fade**
- **When**: Scene endings, time passage
- **Effect**: Smooth fade to/from black
- **Use**: Sparingly, for emphasis

**Dissolve**
- **When**: Time passage, related scenes
- **Effect**: Smooth blend between scenes
- **Use**: Occasionally, for specific effect

**Wipe**
- **When**: Rarely needed
- **Effect**: One scene replaces another
- **Use**: Very rarely, if at all

#### Transition Guidelines
- **Keep It Simple**: Cuts are usually best
- **Be Consistent**: Use same transition style
- **Don't Overuse**: Too many transitions are distracting
- **Match Content**: Transitions should match content tone

### Pacing

#### Maintaining Pace
- **Not Too Fast**: Allow time to see details
- **Not Too Slow**: Keep momentum
- **Varied**: Mix fast and slow sections
- **Natural**: Should feel natural, not forced

#### When to Speed Up
- **Repetitive Actions**: Removing many screws
- **Long Processes**: Time-consuming operations
- **Less Important**: Routine operations
- **Time-Lapse**: Show process without boring viewer

#### When to Slow Down
- **Important Moments**: Key reveals, important points
- **Complex Actions**: Difficult operations
- **Details**: Showing important details
- **Emphasis**: Emphasizing key points

### Color Correction

#### Basic Correction
- **Exposure**: Adjust brightness if needed
- **Contrast**: Enhance contrast for clarity
- **Color Balance**: Correct color casts
- **Saturation**: Adjust if needed (usually keep natural)

#### Goals
- **Consistent**: Consistent look throughout video
- **Natural**: Natural colors, not over-processed
- **Clear**: Clear, visible details
- **Professional**: Professional appearance

#### Tools
- **Basic**: Built-in color correction tools
- **Advanced**: Color grading tools (if needed)
- **Presets**: Use presets for consistency

### Graphics and Text

#### When to Use Graphics
- **Labels**: Label components or features
- **Titles**: Section titles, device name
- **Information**: Key facts, statistics
- **Emphasis**: Highlight important points

#### Text Guidelines
- **Readable**: Large enough to read
- **Clear**: Clear font, good contrast
- **Consistent**: Consistent style throughout
- **Not Distracting**: Don't overpower content

#### Graphic Style
- **Simple**: Clean, simple graphics
- **Professional**: Professional appearance
- **Consistent**: Consistent style
- **Minimal**: Don't overuse

## Audio Editing

### Sync Audio with Video

#### Manual Sync
- **Method**: Align audio with video manually
- **When**: Audio recorded separately
- **How**: Use waveform or clap sync
- **Check**: Verify sync throughout video

#### Auto Sync
- **Method**: Software auto-sync
- **When**: Audio recorded with video
- **How**: Use auto-sync feature
- **Check**: Verify sync is accurate

### Audio Levels

#### Mixing
- **Narration**: Primary audio, clear and audible
- **Background**: Background music (if used), lower volume
- **Sound Effects**: Sound effects (if used), appropriate level
- **Balance**: Balance all audio elements

#### Levels
- **Narration**: -12 dB to -6 dB average
- **Background Music**: -20 dB to -16 dB (under narration)
- **Sound Effects**: Appropriate to content
- **No Clipping**: Never exceed 0 dB

### Audio Processing

#### Basic Processing
- **Noise Reduction**: Remove background noise
- **Normalization**: Adjust overall volume
- **Compression**: Even out volume levels
- **EQ**: Adjust frequency balance

#### Advanced Processing (If Needed)
- **De-essing**: Reduce harsh "s" sounds
- **Reverb Reduction**: Reduce echo
- **Limiting**: Prevent clipping

## Export Settings

### Resolution
- **1080p**: 1920x1080 (standard)
- **4K**: 3840x2160 (if source is 4K)
- **Match Source**: Match source resolution

### Frame Rate
- **30fps**: Standard frame rate
- **60fps**: If source is 60fps
- **Match Source**: Match source frame rate

### Codec
- **H.264**: Standard, widely compatible
- **H.265**: Better compression, newer
- **Format**: MP4 container

### Bitrate
- **1080p**: 10-20 Mbps
- **4K**: 30-50 Mbps
- **Quality**: Higher bitrate = better quality

### Audio
- **Format**: AAC or MP3
- **Bitrate**: 128-192 kbps
- **Sample Rate**: 48 kHz

## Common Editing Mistakes

### ❌ Over-Editing
- **Problem**: Too many effects, transitions
- **Solution**: Keep it simple, let content shine

### ❌ Poor Pacing
- **Problem**: Too fast or too slow
- **Solution**: Review pacing, adjust timing

### ❌ Audio Issues
- **Problem**: Out of sync, wrong levels
- **Solution**: Check sync, adjust levels

### ❌ Inconsistent Quality
- **Problem**: Varying quality throughout
- **Solution**: Color correct, maintain consistency

### ❌ Too Many Graphics
- **Problem**: Graphics overwhelm content
- **Solution**: Use graphics sparingly, keep it minimal

## Project Organization

### Folder Structure
```
project-name/
├── footage/
│   ├── raw/
│   ├── selects/
│   └── b-roll/
├── audio/
│   ├── narration/
│   └── music/
├── graphics/
│   ├── logos/
│   └── text/
├── exports/
│   ├── final/
│   └── drafts/
└── project-files/
    └── [editing software files]
```

### File Naming
- **Descriptive**: Clear, descriptive names
- **Consistent**: Consistent naming convention
- **Organized**: Organized by type/date
- **Example**: `device-intro-001.mp4`, `narration-final.wav`
