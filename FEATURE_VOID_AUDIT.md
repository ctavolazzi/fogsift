# FogSift Feature Void Audit

**Date:** December 27, 2025
**Version:** 1.0
**Purpose:** Identify missing features that similar consulting websites offer and that the target audience would expect

---

## Executive Summary

FogSift is a diagnostic consulting business website targeting mid-to-large organizations facing complex operational challenges. This audit identifies **42 feature gaps** across 8 categories, prioritized by impact on conversion, credibility, and user experience.

**Current State:** Clean, professional single-page site with wiki knowledge base
**Key Gaps:** Trust signals, lead capture, social proof, interactive engagement, booking automation

---

## Target Audience Profile

**Primary Audience:**
- C-suite executives, VPs of Operations, Directors of Strategy
- Mid-to-large companies ($10M-$500M+ revenue)
- Manufacturing, tech, healthcare, professional services
- Age range: 35-55
- Decision-making authority with budget access

**What They Expect:**
- Immediate credibility signals (logos, testimonials, credentials)
- Easy scheduling without back-and-forth emails
- Clear pricing with no surprises
- Proof of results (case studies with numbers)
- Professional, polished experience on par with McKinsey-level firms
- Mobile-first experience (often browsing on phones during commute)

## Priority Matrix

### Must-Have (Do First)

1. **Client testimonials section** - Social proof is non-negotiable
2. **Email capture with lead magnet** - Passive lead generation
3. **Calendly/booking integration** - Reduce friction to zero
4. **LinkedIn profile link** - Basic credibility verification
5. **Analytics (Plausible/Cloudflare)** - Measure everything
6. **Contact form** - Not everyone wants to use email clients

## Risk Assessment

**Without These Features:**
- **Lost leads:** No email capture = visitors leave forever
- **Lost credibility:** No testimonials = "Is this person real?"
- **Lost conversions:** Mailto links = 50%+ drop-off vs contact forms
- **Lost insights:** No analytics = flying blind
- **Lost SEO:** No sitemap = reduced discoverability

**Estimated Impact:** Implementing Phase 1-2 could increase conversion rates by **3-5x** based on industry benchmarks.

---

## Conclusion

FogSift has excellent content and clean architecture but lacks the **conversion infrastructure** that consulting websites need to generate leads. The wiki is a hidden gem that could drive SEO traffic if properly optimized.

**Top 5 Actions to Take Now:**
1. Add client testimonials (even 2-3 is transformative)
2. Embed Calendly booking widget
3. Build email capture with lead magnet
4. Add contact form (not just mailto)
5. Enable analytics

These five changes would transform FogSift from a "digital business card" into a **lead generation engine**.
