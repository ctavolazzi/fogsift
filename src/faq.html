FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Straight answers to common questions.

The Queue

What happens after I pay?

You'll get a confirmation email with a form to describe your problem. Fill it out with as much detail as you can. Your submission enters the queue, and I pick from it daily.

How long until my problem gets picked?

It varies. I pick two per day: one highest-rated by the community, one random. Could be tomorrow, could be next week. The random selection means everyone has a fair shot regardless of when they submitted.

What if my problem never gets picked?

If you've been in the queue for 30 days without being selected, reach out and I'll either work your problem or refund your $20. No one gets left behind.

Can I request anonymity?

Yes. When you submit, you can request that identifying details be obscured. I'll still work the problem publicly (so others can learn), but your name and company won't be visible.

The Work

What kinds of problems do you work on?

Business and operational problems that are stuck. Process bottlenecks, decision paralysis, team dysfunction, unclear strategy, technical architecture questions. If you're spinning your wheels on something, it probably fits.

What if my problem is too big for an hour?

Most problems can at least be clarified in an hour. I'll identify the real issue and outline a path forward. If it needs more work, we can discuss a deeper engagement.

What if you can't solve it?

Not every problem has a clean solution. But every problem can be better understood. I'll show you what I find, even if the answer is "this is harder than it looks" or "here's why you're actually stuck."

Do you give specific advice or just frameworks?

Both. I'll give you concrete recommendations where I can. When I can't, I'll give you the framework to figure it out yourself. No vague platitudes.

The Video

Why do you work on camera?

Transparency. You see exactly how I think through problems. No black box consulting where you get a polished deck with no insight into how we got there. Plus, others can learn from watching.

Where are videos published?

On the FogSift YouTube channel. Each session becomes an episode. You get access to the video plus a written summary.

Can I request my video not be published?

The public format is core to how this works. If your problem requires true confidentiality, the queue isn't the right fit. Consider a private engagement instead.

Money

Why $20?

Low enough to be accessible. High enough to filter for people who are serious. The price isn't the point; the work is.

Do you offer refunds?

If your problem sits in the queue for 30+ days without being picked, yes. If you're dissatisfied with the work, let's talk. I'd rather make it right than keep your money.

What's the catch?

No catch. I genuinely enjoy this work. The low price lets me help more people. The public format means the value compounds as others learn from each session.

Other Options

What if the queue doesn't fit my needs?

Check the offers page for other options: free triage calls, private deep dives, and contract engagements for bigger projects.

Can I just email you a question?

Sure. christopher@fogsift.com. I can't promise a deep answer for free, but I'll point you in the right direction if I can.

Still have questions?

Just ask. I'm a real person.
