What to Include in Your Submission

When you submit on Ko-fi, include in your message:

  1. The problem — What's stuck? Be specific.
  2. Context — What have you tried? What's the situation?
  3. Goal — What would "solved" look like?
  4. Privacy preference — Public (default), anonymous, or private (if applicable)
  5. Contact info — Email where we can send your report

Example submission:

"My Raspberry Pi project keeps crashing after ~2 hours of runtime. I've checked the power supply and it seems fine. Running a Python script that reads sensor data. Want it to run 24/7 reliably. Public is fine. Email: me@example.com"

What You Get

  • PDF Report — Findings, recommendations, and next steps
  • Video Response — Usually 15-90 seconds summarizing what we found
  • Maybe a YouTube Video — If the problem is interesting enough, we might make a full episode

What Makes a Good Submission?

  • Specific — "My 3D prints have layer separation at 40mm height" beats "prints aren't working"
  • Bounded — Something that can be meaningfully addressed in an hour
  • Actionable — You're ready to try what we suggest

What We Don't Do

  • Financial advice or investment questions
  • Legal advice
  • Interpersonal disputes or drama
  • Anything illegal or unethical

Frequently Asked Questions

How long until my problem gets addressed?

We can't give a specific timeline. Could be days, could be weeks. We aim for up to 2 per day but life happens. If you need something faster, reach out directly about consulting.

Can I get a refund?

Yes. Request a refund through Ko-fi if we haven't addressed your request yet, or if you change your mind. No hard feelings.

Will my problem be public?

By default, yes — so others can learn. You can request anonymization (we obscure identifying details) or mention if you need privacy. Note: we can't guarantee complete privacy for anything submitted online.

What if you can't solve my problem?

That's part of it. We'll document what we tried, what we learned, and point you in the right direction. The value is in the process, not just the outcome.
