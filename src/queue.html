How It Works

1

Submit Your Problem

Pay $20, describe your problem, and you're in the queue. You'll see your position on this page.

2

Wait for Your Turn

We pick 2 problems per day - one from the top, one at random. Each gets up to 1 hour of focused attention.

3

Get Your Results

If a video is published, it will appear on FogSift YouTube. You also receive a direct reply via your requested contact method.

4

Want It Faster?

Use the bump option to move up in the queue. One bump per day keeps it fair.

5

Need Something Custom?

The queue is for quick hits. For deeper work, check out our custom work requests.

About Public Work Pages

By default, your work session is public. Your problem and our solution get documented here for others to learn from. This transparency is what makes FogSift work.

Want privacy? Request anonymization or "no public page" when you submit. Must be specified at submission time.
