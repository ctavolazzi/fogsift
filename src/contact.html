What to Include

When you reach out, it helps to know:

  • What's the problem you're trying to solve?
  • What have you already tried?
  • What would success look like?
  • Any privacy constraints or anonymization requests?

Don't worry if you don't have all the answers. That's what we figure out together.

What Happens Next

We review submissions and pick what to work on next. You get a PDF write-up and video response, plus one of three outcomes:

Quick Answers

What if you can't solve my problem?

You still get the PDF write-up and video response, plus a narrower problem statement or referral. The $20 covers the session, not a guaranteed fix.

How long until I hear back?

We review submissions daily and work through the queue in order. Most submissions are addressed within a week.

Is my submission confidential?

We may publish a video response. Your submission may be used for marketing. You can request anonymization at any time.

Can I get a refund?

If we haven't started work yet, yes. Once we begin the session, the $20 is non-refundable. See our Terms.
