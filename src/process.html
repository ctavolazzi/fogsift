Why This Works

A Focus Session is a full-on research squad (me + AI + friends + network) focused on your submission for up to an hour. Worth way more than $20, kept low so anyone can access it. You walk away with actionable output.

What You Always Get

  • The Video Response - A response scoped to your submission
  • The PDF Write-up - Written findings, what we tried, what we learned
  • Direction - One of three outcomes with clear next steps

The Three Outcomes

  • "Let's go deeper." - We want to work on this more with you
  • "I know people." - We can connect you with the right expert
  • "This is sick." - You should do this, here's how I'd approach it

Will my submission be public?

We may or may not publish a video. Your submission may be used for other purposes, including marketing. Request anonymization if needed.

Why Daily?

Once a day, we review everything and decide what we can take on next. That balance keeps the queue fair.
