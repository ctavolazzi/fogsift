FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is PaperBin SaaS?

PaperBin SaaS is a curated library of premium PDF templates. Our AI system (WAFT) generates professional drafts, which are then polished by human designers. The result: quality-controlled PDFs ready for your business.

How does PaperBin SaaS work?

PaperBin SaaS uses a hybrid AI-human workflow: (1) WAFT generates a professional PDF draft using advanced templates, (2) A human designer reviews and polishes the document in professional software, (3) The final PDF is quality-checked and added to our library for instant download.

What makes PaperBin SaaS different from other PDF services?

Unlike AI-only solutions, every PaperBin SaaS template is human-polished. Unlike custom design services, our templates are available instantly at affordable prices. We combine AI efficiency with human quality control.

What is WAFT?

WAFT is our AI-powered document generation system. It uses advanced templates and intelligent layout algorithms to create professional PDF drafts. WAFT handles the heavy lifting so human designers can focus on polish and refinement.
